How it works

Lantos has modernized, streamlined and elevated the process for obtaining ear “impressions” and creating personalized hearing products.

The Lantos System uses patented technology to create a precise 3D digital rendering of the unique geometry of the ear, from the outer ear through the The Lantos Membrane, which mounts onto the tip of the Lantos Scanner, is inserted into the ear canal where it quickly and safely

inflates until it conforms to the patient’s unique ear contours. The interior of the inflated membrane provides a uniform surface and controlled environment for scanning; enabling the rapid capture of over 1,000,000 data points per ear.

The disposable membrane is designed for single-patient use, which ensures maximum hygiene and patient safety during the process.
